Important: This tool estimates risk for Type 2 diabetes only — it does not assess Type 1 diabetes, gestational diabetes, or other forms. Risk scores are generated by a logistic regression model trained on CDC BRFSS survey data and reflect population-level statistical associations; some degree of inaccuracy is inherent in any predictive model. This is not a clinical diagnosis. The only way to confirm prediabetes or diabetes is a lab-based test such as an A1C (hemoglobin A1C), fasting plasma glucose, or oral glucose tolerance test, interpreted by a qualified healthcare provider. Please discuss your results with a provider to determine appropriate next steps for your individual situation.
