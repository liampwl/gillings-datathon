About This Tool

This demonstration tool was created using public health survey data from the Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System (BRFSS). BRFSS collects information from hundreds of thousands of Americans each year about:

  • Health behaviors
  • Chronic conditions
  • Preventive health care

Our model uses selected BRFSS indicators to show how population health data can be translated into meaningful feedback for individuals.

Privacy by Design

We believe in strict data minimization. Your health inputs are transmitted to a stateless server strictly to calculate your score—no databases are connected, and your data is never logged, stored, or sold. The only information sent to a third party is your ZIP Code (optional), which is pinged to the official Health Resources & Services Administration (HRSA) API to retrieve nearby public health clinics.

For Research & Demonstration Only. This tool is part of the Gillings School of Global Public Health Datathon 2026 and is intended for educational and research purposes.

Important: This tool estimates risk for Type 2 diabetes only — it does not assess Type 1 diabetes, gestational diabetes, or other forms. Risk scores are generated by a logistic regression model trained on CDC BRFSS survey data and reflect population-level statistical associations; some degree of inaccuracy is inherent in any predictive model. This is not a clinical diagnosis. The only way to confirm prediabetes or diabetes is a lab-based test such as an A1C (hemoglobin A1C), fasting plasma glucose, or oral glucose tolerance test, interpreted by a qualified healthcare provider. Please discuss your results with a provider to determine appropriate next steps for your individual situation.
